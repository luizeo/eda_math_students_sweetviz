# Attributes for student-mat.csv (Math course) dataset:
1 school(Escola) - student's school (binary: "GP" - Gabriel Pereira or "MS" - Mousinho da Silveira)
2 sex(Genero) - student's sex (binary: "F" - female or "M" - male)
3 age (Idade) - student's age (numeric: from 15 to 22)
4 address (Endereco) - student's home address type (binary: "U" - urban or "R" - rural)
5 famsize (TamFamilia) - family size (binary: "LE3" - less or equal to 3 or "GT3" - greater than 3)
6 Pstatus (StatusPais) - parent's cohabitation status (binary: "T" - living together or "A" - apart)
7 Medu (InstruçaoMae) - mother's education (numeric: 0 - none,  1 - primary education (4th grade), 2 â 5th to 9th grade, 3 â secondary education or 4 â higher education)
8 Fedu (InstruçãoPai) - father's education (numeric: 0 - none,  1 - primary education (4th grade), 2 â 5th to 9th grade, 3 â secondary education or 4 â higher education)
9 Mjob (OcupaçãoMae) - mother's job (nominal: "teacher", "health" care related, civil "services" (e.g. administrative or police), "at_home" or "other")
10 Fjob (OcupaçãoPai)- father's job (nominal: "teacher", "health" care related, civil "services" (e.g. administrative or police), "at_home" or "other")
11 reason (MotivoEscolha)- reason to choose this school (nominal: close to "home", school "reputation", "course" preference or "other")
12 guardian (Guarda) - student's guardian (nominal: "mother", "father" or "other")
13 traveltime (TempoViagemEscola)- home to school travel time (numeric: 1 - <15 min., 2 - 15 to 30 min., 3 - 30 min. to 1 hour, or 4 - >1 hour)
14 studytime (TempoEstudoSemanal) - weekly study time (numeric: 1 - <2 hours, 2 - 2 to 5 hours, 3 - 5 to 10 hours, or 4 - >10 hours)
15 failures (Reprovacoes) - number of past class failures (numeric: n if 1<=n<3, else 4)
16 schoolsup (AjudaExtraEscola) - extra educational support (binary: yes or no)
17 famsup (AjudaFamiliar) - family educational support (binary: yes or no)
18 paid (AulasParticulares)- extra paid classes within the course subject (Math or Portuguese) (binary: yes or no)
19 activities (AtividadesExtra)- extra-curricular activities (binary: yes or no)
20 nursery (FezMaternal) - attended nursery school (binary: yes or no)
21 higher (QuerFaculdade) - wants to take higher education (binary: yes or no)
22 internet - Internet access at home (binary: yes or no)
23 romantic (EmUmRelacionamento) - with a romantic relationship (binary: yes or no)
24 famrel (RelacaoFamiliar) - quality of family relationships (numeric: from 1 - very bad to 5 - excellent)
25 freetime (TempoLivre) - free time after school (numeric: from 1 - very low to 5 - very high)
26 goout (SaiComAmigos) - going out with friends (numeric: from 1 - very low to 5 - very high)
27 Dalc (ConsumoAlcoolDiaUtil) - workday alcohol consumption (numeric: from 1 - very low to 5 - very high)
28 Walc - (ConsumoAlcoolFimSemana) weekend alcohol consumption (numeric: from 1 - very low to 5 - very high)
29 health (SitSaude)- current health status (numeric: from 1 - very bad to 5 - very good)
30 absences (Faltas) - number of school absences (numeric: from 0 to 93)

# these grades are related with the course subject, Math or Portuguese:
31 G1 - first period grade (numeric: from 0 to 20)
31 G2 - second period grade (numeric: from 0 to 20)
32 G3 - final grade (numeric: from 0 to 20, output target)
